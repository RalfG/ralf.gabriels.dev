Ralf Gabriels

Postdoctoral researcher

CompOmics @ VIB-UGent Center for Medical Biotechnology, Ghent, BE

Biography

I obtained my MSc degree in Biomedical Sciences at Ghent University in 2017 after completing a bioinformatics thesis project in the CompOmics group. In January 2018, I continued the work of my master thesis as an FWO-SB PhD fellow under the supervision of prof. Sven Degroeve and prof. Lennart Martens. In May 2022, I graduated as Doctor in Life Sciences and was granted a Junior Postdoctoral Fellowship by FWO, starting in October 2022.

My research involves investigating novel applications of machine learning in proteomics, with a focus on peptide identification. I am also a member of the European Bioinformatics Community for Mass Spectrometry organizational committee and I contribute to the development of community standards in computational mass spectrometry at HUPO-PSI.
